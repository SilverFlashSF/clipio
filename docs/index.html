Privacy Policy for Clipio

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Clipio ("Application") for mobile devices. The Application is a lightweight clipboard history manager designed to help users organize and recall their copied content.

1. Personal Data and Collection

Clipio values your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data or clipboard contents.

All clipboard history captured by the Application is stored locally and exclusively on your device. None of this data is uploaded to our servers, any cloud services, or shared with third parties.

2. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

The Application contains advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain device-specific parameters to serve relevant ads. This data collection is governed by Google's privacy policy.

For more information, please review the privacy policies of the third-party providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

3. Device Permissions

To function correctly, the Application requires the following system permissions:

  • Accessibility Service: Used to monitor system-wide copy operations locally to record clipboard history automatically. No data read by this service is ever sent off your device.
  • Notifications: Used to deliver background updates and alerts when clipping is running.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the email address listed in the Google Play Console store registration.
